# Void Reckoning Faction Comparison

| Faction | Archetype | Strengths | Weaknesses | Playstyle |
|---------|-----------|-----------|------------|-----------|
| **Templars of the Flux** | Crusader (Will/Aether/Mass) | Morale Immune, Psychic Defense, Melee | Low Mobility, Unbalanced Stats | Aggressive, Fanatical Charges |
| **Transcendent Order** | Resonator (Aether/Will/Focus) | Extreme Psychic Power, Control | Very Fragile, Low Physical Dmg | Glass Cannon, Manipulation |
| **Steel-Bound Syndicate** | Industrial (Mass/Cohesion/Energy) | Incredible Armor & Durability, Artillery | Slow Speed, No Psychic | Defensive Turtle, Attrition |
| **Bio-Tide Collective** | Biological (Mass/Freq/Cohesion) | Numbers, Regen, Fast Reproduction | Low Armor, Uncoordinated | Swarm Overrun, Biomass Eco |
| **Algorithmic Hierarchy** | Logical (Info/Stability/Energy) | Accuracy, Reanimation, Efficiency | Susceptible to Logic Hacks, Slow | Methodical, Inevitable Push |
| **Nebula Drifters** | Speed (Freq/Volatility/Focus) | Extreme Speed, Hit-and-Run, Stealth | Paper Thin Armor, Low Discipline | Raiding, Kiting, Ambush |
| **Aurelian Hegemony** | Technological (Energy/Focus/Info) | Balanced Stats, Diplomacy, Energy Weapons | No specific mastery (Jack of all trades) | Flexible, Combined Arms |
| **Void-Spawn Entities** | Aetheric (Aether/Volatility/Will) | Reality Fluxing, Terror, Invulnerable | Unstable (take dmg over time), Banishable | chaotic, High Risk/Reward |
| **Scrap-Lord Marauders** | Chaotic (Volatility/Mass/Freq) | Unpredictable, Loot Bonuses, Cheap | Unreliable Tech, Low Accuracy | Hordey, Rolling Momentum |
| **Primeval Sentinels** | Elite (Focus/Will/Aether) | Mastery of all stats, Ancient Tech | Extremely Expensive, Few Units | Elite Quality over Quantity |

## Key Archetype Matchups

*   **Crusader vs Aetheric**: Crusaders have high Will to resist Aetheric powers. *Advantage: Crusader.*
*   **Industrial vs Biological**: Industrial armor resists biological claws, but swarms can overwhelm single targets. *Tie.*
*   **Speed vs Logical**: Corsairs can outmaneuver the slow Cyber machines, but Cyber accuracy counters Evasion. *Skill Matchup.*
*   **Resonator vs Industrial**: Resonators bypass armor with Aether attacks, destroying the Steel-Bound Syndicate's main defense. *Advantage: Resonator.*
