# Nebula Drifters Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Training Pit
- **Cost:** 600 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 10/turn
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Corsair Raider], [Void Reaver]
- **Effects:** 
  - +5 Morale
  - +10% Exp Gain from combat
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Rough arenas where pirates prove their worth or die trying.

### Tier 2: Chop Shop
- **Cost:** 1800 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 40/turn
- **Build Time:** 4 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Raider Skiff], [Looted Tank]
- **Effects:**
  - +30% Vehicle production speed
  - Salvages 25% of cost from destroyed enemies
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_Nebula_Drifters_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** Where stolen vehicles are retrofitted with more guns and engines.
