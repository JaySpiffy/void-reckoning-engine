# Bio-Tide Collective Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Spawning Pool
- **Cost:** 700 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 15/turn
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Hive Drone], [Warrior Strain]
- **Effects:** 
  - +30% Growth rate
  - -25% Infantry cost
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Nutritious slurry pools where lesser bio-forms are birthed in the thousands.

### Tier 2: Gestation Chamber
- **Cost:** 1800 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 40/turn
- **Build Time:** 4 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Chitinous Behemoth Strain], [Bio-Tank]
- **Effects:**
  - +20% Monstrous Creature production speed
  - +10% Unit HP
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_BioTide_Collective_Industrial_Forging] (Evolutionary Adaptation)
- **Lore:** Pulsing sacs where massive bio-titans are grown from accelerated gene-stock.

## Economic Buildings

### Tier 1: Digestion Pool
- **Cost:** 600 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 10/turn
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Effects:**
  - +100 Requisition/turn

- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Acidic pits that break down organic matter to fuel the fleet.
