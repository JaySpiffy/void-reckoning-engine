# Transcendent Order Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Meditation Chamber
- **Cost:** 800 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 20/turn
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Ascended Initiate], [Ascended Disciple]
- **Effects:** 
  - +10 Resonator Power (Planet)
  - +1 Rank for Resonator recruits
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Quiet halls where aspiring psions learn to control their latent abilities.

### Tier 2: Crystal Foundry
- **Cost:** 2000 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 50/turn
- **Build Time:** 5 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Grav-Tank], [Prism Crawler]
- **Effects:**
  - +20% Vehicle production speed
  - +5% Shield strength for new units
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_Transcendent_Order_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** Focuses aetheric energy into physical matter to sing vehicles into existence.
