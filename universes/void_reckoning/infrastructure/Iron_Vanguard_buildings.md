# Steel-Bound Syndicate Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Drill Yard
- **Cost:** 800 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 20/turn
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Vanguard Trooper], [Heavy Gunner]
- **Effects:** 
  - +10 Planetary Defense
  - +1 Rank for Heavy Infantry
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Brutal training grounds where weak recruits are forged into iron soldiers.

### Tier 2: Heavy Assembly Plant
- **Cost:** 2000 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 50/turn
- **Build Time:** 5 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Siege Tank], [Artillery Platform]
- **Effects:**
  - +25% Vehicle production speed
  - -15% Building Cost (Local)
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_SteelBound_Syndicate_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** Massive automated factories churning out the heaviest tanks in the galaxy.
