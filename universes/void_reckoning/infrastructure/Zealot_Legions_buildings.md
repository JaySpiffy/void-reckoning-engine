# Templars of the Flux Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Chapel-Barracks
- **Cost:** 800 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 20/turn
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Unlocks:** [Initiate], [Templar]
- **Capacity:** 10 Squads
- **Effects:** 
  - +15 Morale (Planet-wide)
  - +1 Rank for Infantry recruits
  - +5 Planetary Defense
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Sacred training grounds where initiates learn to channel conviction into combat prowess.

### Tier 2: Pattern Manufactorum
- **Cost:** 2000 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 50/turn
- **Build Time:** 5 turns
- **Unlocks:** [Chariot], [Purifier], [Sorrow-Walker]
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Effects:**
  - +20% Vehicle production speed
  - -10% Vehicle maintenance cost
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_Templars_of_the_Flux_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** Massive assembly lines blessed by tech-priests to produce the engines of holy war.

## Special Buildings

### Tier 3: Orbital Cathedral
- **Cost:** 4000 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 150/turn
- **Build Time:** 8 turns
- **Unlocks:** [Sword Class], [Cathedral Class], [Basilica Class]
- **Capacity:** 3 Capital Ships
- **Effects:**
  - Unlocks Space Ship Construction
  - +10% Ship Fire Rate (Orbit)
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_Templars_of_the_Flux_Void_Shielding]
- **Lore:** A space station of immense size, serving as both shipyard and temple in the void.

- **Cost:** 5000 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 100/turn
- **Build Time:** 7 turns

- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_Templars_of_the_Flux_Conviction_Doctrines]
- **Lore:** A monument to the Void Reckoning, where faith becomes a tangible force of reality.
