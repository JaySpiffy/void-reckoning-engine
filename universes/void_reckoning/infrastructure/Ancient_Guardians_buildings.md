# Primeval Sentinels Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Aspect Shrine

- **Cost:** 900 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 25/turn
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Unlocks:** [Guardian], [Warden]
- **Capacity:** 10 Squads
- **Effects:**
  - +20% Exp Gain
  - +1 Rank for Infantry
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Temples dedicated to the specific facets of war, training warriors to perfection.

### Tier 2: Gravitic Forge

- **Cost:** 2500 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 60/turn
- **Build Time:** 5 turns
- **Unlocks:** [Grav-Sled], [Stalker]
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Effects:**
  - +15% Vehicle Speed
  - +10% Vehicle Shielding
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_Primeval_Sentinels_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** Manipulates gravity to assemble sleek, hovering war machines.
