# Void-Spawn Entities Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Summoning Circle
- **Cost:** 700 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 10/turn
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Lesser Daemon], [Rift Hound]
- **Effects:** 
  - +20% Summon speed
  - -10% Army Upkeep
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** A tear in reality marked with runes, allowing lesser entities to slip through.

### Tier 2: Flux Forge
- **Cost:** 2000 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 50/turn
- **Build Time:** 5 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Soul Grinder], [Daemon Engine]
- **Effects:**
  - +25% Daemon Engine production
  - +10% Unit HP
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_VoidSpawn_Entities_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** Fuses metal and flesh with pure flux energy to create living machines.
