# Aurelian Hegemony Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Military Academy
- **Cost:** 800 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 20/turn
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Solar Marine], [Hegemony Guard]
- **Effects:** 
  - +2 Rank for Infantry recruits
  - +10% Army Morale
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** High-prestige institutions teaching strategy, discipline, and loyalty.

### Tier 2: Arsenals of Sol
- **Cost:** 2200 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 60/turn
- **Build Time:** 5 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Solar Tank], [Artillery Walker]
- **Effects:**
  - +20% Vehicle production speed
  - +10% Weapon Damage
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_Aurelian_Hegemony_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** The finest weapons factories in the galaxy, producing precision ordinance.

## Special Buildings
