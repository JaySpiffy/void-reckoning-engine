# Scrap-Lord Marauders Infrastructure

## Military Buildings

### Tier 1: Fight Pit
- **Cost:** 500 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 5/turn
- **Build Time:** 2 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Clan Raiders], [Brawlers]
- **Effects:** 
  - +10% Army Damage
  - +5 Morale
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Lore:** Where the biggest and strongest prove they're ready for the Furor!

### Tier 2: Mek Shop
- **Cost:** 1500 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 30/turn
- **Build Time:** 4 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Scrap Trukk], [Battle Wagon]
- **Effects:**
  - +30% Vehicle production speed
  - +15% Fire rate (Random variance)
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_ScrapLord_Marauders_Industrial_Forging]
- **Lore:** The domain of Mekraiders who hammer scraps into deadly, if unreliable, tanks.

## Economic Buildings

- **Build Time:** 1 turns

## Special Buildings

### Tier 3: Space Hulk Dock
- **Cost:** 3000 Requisition
- **Maintenance:** 80/turn
- **Build Time:** 6 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
- **Unlocks:** [Scavenger Frigate], [Scavenger Cruiser]
- **Effects:**
  - Unlocks Space Ship Construction
  - +20% Ship Hull Points
- **Prerequisites:** [Tech_ScrapLord_Marauders_Void_Shielding]

- **Maintenance:** 10/turn
- **Build Time:** 3 turns
- **Capacity:** 5 Units
