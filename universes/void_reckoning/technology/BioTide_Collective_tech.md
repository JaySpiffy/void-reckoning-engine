# Bio-Tide Collective Technology

## Tier 1

### Basic Doctrine

- **Cost:** 1000
- **Name:** Synaptic Imperative
- **Description:** The overriding will of the Hive Mind directing the swarm.
- **Effects:** +10% Cohesion, Unlocks T1 Gaunts.

### Logistics

- **Cost:** 1000
- **Name:** Metabolic Overdrive
- **Description:** Accelerating the metabolism of carrier beasts.
- **Effects:** +5% Army Speed.

### Industrial Forging

- **Cost:** 800
- **Name:** Rapid Gestation
- **Description:** Spawning pools churn out bio-forms at alarming rates.
- **Effects:** +10% Unit Production Speed.

### Resource Extraction

- **Cost:** 800
- **Name:** Biomass Consumption
- **Description:** Breaking down local flora and fauna into raw slurry.
- **Effects:** +10% Biomass/Requisition Income.

- **Cost:** 2500
- **Name:** Bio-Plasma Symbiotes

- **Cost:** 3000
- **Name:** Narvhal Instincts

### Trade Networks

- **Cost:** 2000
- **Name:** Mycelial Spreading

- **Effects:** +20% Income from infested worlds.
