# Void Reckoning: Technology Tree Philosophy

This document outlines the strategic design and core identity of each faction's technology progression.

## Overview
The tech tree is divided into 3 Tiers, costing roughly 1000, 2500, and 5000+ resources respectively. While the structure (Nodes) is shared, the *Flavor* and *Strategic Bonuses* are unique to each faction, driven by their DNA.

## Faction Philosophies

### Templars of the Flux
*   **Theme**: *Faith as Armor*. Technology is a vehicle for religious expression.
*   **Focus**: Morale, Infantry Swarms, Defensive Structures.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Aegis of Faith* (Planetary Shielding powered by belief).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Saintly Ascension* - Turning heroes into immortal beacons.

### Bio-Tide Collective
*   **Theme**: *Biological Evolution*. "Technology" is just directed mutation.
*   **Focus**: Unit Production Speed, Biomass Efficiency, Organic Ships.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Narvhal Instincts* (Gravity-based travel without engines).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Apex Evolution* - Instant adaptation to enemy damage types.

### Steel-Bound Syndicate
*   **Theme**: *Endurance & Industry*. Steel walls and massive guns.
*   **Focus**: Vehicle Health, Artillery Range, Fortification Strength.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Ferrocrete Casting* (Instant fortress deployment).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Steel Unbroken* - Units become immune to morale shocks.

### Algorithmic Hierarchy
*   **Theme**: *Optimization*. cold logic and AI efficiency.
*   **Focus**: Accuracy, Economic Output, Automaton production.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Calculated Jumps* (Zero scatter flux travel).
*   **T3 Goal**: *The Singularity* - Merging biological and digital consciousness.

### Void-Spawn Entities
*   **Theme**: *Corruption*. Fluxing reality and harvesting souls.
*   **Focus**: Shield Regeneration (Flux saves), Summoning speed, Corruption spreading.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Impossible Geometries* (Demon Engines).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Daemonhood* - Leaders ascend to become Greater Daemons.

### Aurelian Hegemony
*   **Theme**: *Command & Control*. Disciplined ranks and massive fleets.
*   **Focus**: Fleet Cap, Tax Income, Discipline.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Pax Aurelianis* (Global stability boost).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Apocalypse Class* - Unlocking the largest standard battleships in the galaxy.

### Nebula Drifters
*   **Theme**: *Speed & Stealth*. Hit and run tactics.
*   **Focus**: Evasion, Speed, Looting/Raiding income.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Star-Lattice Gates* (Bypassing defenses).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Shadow Fields* - Making entire planets invisible to sensors.

### Transcendent Order
*   **Theme**: *Perfection*. Ancient mastery of psychic arts.
*   **Focus**: Unit XP, Evasion, Psychic Power.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Wraithbone Singing* (Self-repairing structures).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Ynnead's Awakening* - Resurrecting fallen units on the battlefield.

### Primeval Sentinels
*   **Theme**: *Geomancy*. Channeling the planet's own power.
*   **Focus**: Magic/Psychic resistance, Beam weaponry, Teleportation.
*   **Unique Tech**: *The Great Plan* (Strategic map bonuses).
*   **T3 Goal**: *Awakening* - Summoning Lord Kroak-tier spellcasters.

### Scrap-Lord Marauders
*   **Theme**: *Chaos & Loot*. If it works, it works.
*   **Focus**: Cheap units, Scrap income, Random chaotic bonuses.
*   **Unique Tech**: *Tellyporta Tech* (High risk, high reward movement).
*   **T3 Goal**: *The Great Furor!* - Global offensive buff that scales with army size.
