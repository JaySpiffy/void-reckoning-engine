# Void Reckoning Trait System Guide

This guide details the passive trait system for the Void Reckoning universe. Traits are assigned to units based on their **Faction Affinity**.

## System Overview

- **Passive Effects**: Traits provide always-on bonuses (stat mods, immunities, behaviors).
- **Faction Affinity**: Units are more likely to manifest traits associated with their faction's archetype.

## Trait Categories

### 1. Defensive
Traits that increase survivability.

| Trait | Effect | Affinity |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Regeneration** | 5% HP regen/turn | Bio-Tide Collective, Primeval Sentinels |
| **Feel No Pain** | 33% dmg ignore | Templars of the Flux, Steel-Bound Syndicate |
| **Shield Regen** | 10% shield regen/turn | Aurelian Hegemony, Algorithmic Hierarchy |
| **Damage Reduction** | -20% dmg taken | Steel-Bound Syndicate, Algorithmic Hierarchy |
| **Adaptive Armor** | +10% resist to last dmg | Algorithmic Hierarchy, Primeval Sentinels |
| **Ethereal Form** | 25% phase chance | Void-Spawn Entities, Transcendent Order |

### 2. Offensive
Traits that boost damage output.

| Trait | Effect | Affinity |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Armor Piercing** | Ignore 30% armor | Aurelian Hegemony, Primeval Sentinels |
| **Critical Strike** | 15% chance 2x dmg | Nebula Drifters, Scrap-Lord Marauders |
| **Anti-Infantry** | +50% vs Infantry | Bio-Tide Collective, Nebula Drifters |
| **Anti-Vehicle** | +50% vs Vehicle | Steel-Bound Syndicate, Aurelian Hegemony |
| **Splash Damage** | Hit adjacent units | Scrap-Lord Marauders, Void-Spawn Entities |
| **Rending** | Bypass shields | Templars of the Flux, Void-Spawn Entities |

### 3. Mobility
Traits that enhance movement and deployment.

| Trait | Effect | Affinity |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Fleet** | +25% Speed | Nebula Drifters, Bio-Tide Collective |
| **Jump Pack** | Ignore Terrain | Templars of the Flux, Nebula Drifters |
| **Deep Strike** | Deploy Anywhere | Void-Spawn Entities, Nebula Drifters |
| **Infiltrate** | Forward Deploy | Nebula Drifters, Transcendent Order |
| **Relentless** | Move & Shoot | Algorithmic Hierarchy, Steel-Bound Syndicate |

### 4. Morale
Traits that affect leadership and psychology.

| Trait | Effect | Affinity |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Fearless** | Immune to Morale | Templars of the Flux, Bio-Tide Collective |
| **Stubborn** | -50% Morale Dmg | Steel-Bound Syndicate, Primeval Sentinels |
| **Inspiring** | +10% Ally Morale | Templars of the Flux, Primeval Sentinels |
| **Zealot** | +20% Dmg at <50% HP | Templars of the Flux, Void-Spawn Entities |

### 5. Special
Unique and powerful abilities, usually restricted to Heroes or Elites.

| Trait | Effect | Affinity |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Stealth** | Invisible >50% range | Nebula Drifters, Transcendent Order |
| **Eternal Warrior** | No Instant Death | Primeval Sentinels, Templars of the Flux |
| **Synapse** | Command Aura | Bio-Tide Collective |
| **WAAAGH** | +10% per Ally | Scrap-Lord Marauders, Bio-Tide Collective |

## Synergies & Strategy

- **Speed Freak**: Combine `Fleet` + `Critical Strike` for hit-and-run flankers.
- **Tank**: Combine `Damage Reduction` + `Feel No Pain` for an immovable object.
- **Assassin**: Combine `Stealth` + `Infiltrate` + `Rending` to snipe high-value targets.
